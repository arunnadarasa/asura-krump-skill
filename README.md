# Asura Krump Skill

This repository contains the Asura Krump skill, a comprehensive knowledge base and personality profile for Asura (Arun Nadarasa), also known as Ryuazuki - the "Angel of Indian Krump".

## About Asura

Asura is a Krump legend with a black belt in Judo and a background in pharmacy. He is recognized for his contributions to Krump culture globally and in India, where he founded several crews including Desi Buck and Gully Ranis. He was bestowed the title of Prince Yarjack in June 2023.
